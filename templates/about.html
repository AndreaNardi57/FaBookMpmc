About Library Management System

Our Mission

The Library Management System is designed to streamline book management for libraries, schools, and book collections. Our goal is to provide a simple, efficient, and user-friendly platform for tracking and managing books.

Key Features
  • Easy book addition and management
  • Comprehensive book tracking
  • Simple and intuitive interface
  • SQLite database for lightweight storage
